AR lavaFlow

When a volcano erupts, the lava produced is unstoppable. It moves so slowly you can outrun it, but with the density of molten rock, and a temperature of over 1000 degrees Celsius, it will destroy everything in its path. This path being the steepest possible down a mountain. Your goal is to draw a mountain on a piece of paper. Scan it and place targets on the map where you predict the lava will reach.
